RETIREMENT PLANNING, GOLD, AND CRYPTO IN INDIA
=================================================

RETIREMENT PLANNING — THE 3.5% RULE FOR INDIA
------------------------------------------------
Need 28-30x your annual expenses as retirement corpus.
Example: ₹1L/month expenses → need ₹3.4 Cr corpus at retirement.
Start early: ₹10K/month SIP from age 25 at 12% = ₹3.5 Cr by 55.
Same from age 35 = only ₹1.0 Cr. Power of compounding matters.

NPS (NATIONAL PENSION SYSTEM) — LATEST RULES
===============================================
Tax Benefits:
- Section 80CCD(1): Up to ₹1.5L within 80C limit
- Section 80CCD(1B): Additional ₹50K over 80C limit
- Section 80CCD(2): Employer contribution up to 14% (Central Govt) or 10%

At Maturity (Age 60):
- 60% lump sum: TAX-FREE (Budget 2024)
- 40%: Must buy annuity (taxable as income)

Partial Withdrawal: After 3 years, max 25% own contributions, for education/illness/home.
Early Exit: After 5 years. 20% lump sum + 80% annuity.

Asset Allocation: Equity (E) up to 75%, Corporate Bonds (C), Govt Securities (G), Alternative (A) up to 5%.

SENIOR CITIZEN BENEFITS
=========================
- 80TTB: ₹50K deduction on interest income
- Higher FD rates: +0.25-0.50%
- SCSS: 8.2%, 5-year, max ₹30L, 80C eligible

GOLD INVESTMENT OPTIONS
=========================
1. SGB (Sovereign Gold Bonds): BEST option. 2.5% annual interest + gold price. Tax-free LTCG at maturity. 8-year tenure, exit after 5th year. Min 1g, max 4kg/year.
2. Gold ETF: Listed on exchange, 0.5-1% expense ratio. LTCG 12.5% above ₹1.25L.
3. Digital Gold: Via apps, no regulation, capital gains apply.
4. Physical Gold: NOT recommended — making charges 8-25%, storage issues.
Gold should be max 5-10% of portfolio.

CRYPTO TAXATION (EFFECTIVE APRIL 2022)
=========================================
- 30% flat tax on ALL gains (no short/long term distinction)
- 1% TDS on transactions above ₹10K
- No deductions except cost of acquisition
- Crypto losses CANNOT offset any other income or other crypto gains
- No indexation benefit
- Mining/staking: Taxable at slab rate
- Must report in ITR Schedule VDA
